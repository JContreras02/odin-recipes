Hotdogs Recipe

This is a recipe list of ingredients needed to make delicious hotdogs, my way.

Note: Some of these ingredients can be interchangeable and changed to your liking.

Ingredients:

  • Hotdog Buns.
  • Wieners
  • Mayo
  • Mustard
  • Ketchup
  • Onion
  • Oil
  • Paprika
  • Garlic Salt
  • Pepper

Steps:

  1. Start by cutting up half an onion.
  2. Grab a pan and oil it up.
  3. Turn on stove and let oil heat up.
  4. Cut slits into wiener to help it cook.
  5. Slide wieners into pan and move them around so they cook evenly.
  6. As wieners finish cooking, apply seasonings.
  7. Next up, grab onions and toss into left over oil and seasoning until caramilized.
  8. Grab buns and apply mayo to it with kitchen knife or spoon.
  9. Toast buns on pan previously used, by placing the inside of them on pan.
  10. Place your buns on a plate and put wiener in bun.
  11. Apply onions, mustard, and ketchup on your hotdog.
  12. Now you finally have your delicious hotdogs and are ready to eat!
