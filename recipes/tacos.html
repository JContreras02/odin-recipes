Tacos Recipe

This is a recipe list of ingredients needed to make delicious tacos, my way.

Note: Some of these ingredients can be interchangeable and changed to your liking.

Ingredients:

  • Corn Tortillas
  • Steak (Of your choice)
  • Lemon
  • Cilantro
  • Onion
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. First start by cutting up meat, add lemon and choice of seasoning.
  2. Let marinate as you finish all other tasks.
  3. Next up, start by dicing onions and cilantro.
  4. Cut up some lemons in either halves or quarters.
  5. Come back to your meat and start by oiling a pan/ grill and letting it warm up.
  6. After it warms up begin to add meat and move meat around so it cooks evenly.
  7. Place meat in pan.
  8. Grab choice of tortillas.
  9. Oil up pan/ grill and place tortillas, make sure to flip them every so often.
  10. Place tortillas on plate and grab spoon and add meat to your tortillas.
  11. Add cilantro and onions that you had diced earlier.
  12. Finally add salsa and lemon to your tacos.
  13. Now you finally have your delicious tacos and are ready to eat!
