Pizza Recipe

This is a recipe list of ingredients needed to make delicious pizza, my way.

Note: Some of these ingredients can be interchangeable and changed to your liking.

Ingredients:

  • Dough
  • Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • Pepperoni

Steps:

  1. First, preheat oven to 475.
  2. Here is a pizza dough recipe as i usually buy one already made.
  3. Mix melted butter with minced garlic in bowl to make garlic butter for crust.
  4. Start by using a spoon to apply marinara sauce in a circular direction on the dough.
  5. Apply as much cheese as you'd like to your pizza.
  6. Place pepperoni around pizza.
  7. Next up, grab your garlic butter and use a brush to apply it around the crust of the pizza.
  8. Place pizza into oven for 15, while consistenly checking to make sure it doesn't burn.
  9. After your pizza finally looks ready in the oven, take it down and let it cool for about 5 minutes.
  10. Now you finally have your delicious pizza and are ready to eat!
